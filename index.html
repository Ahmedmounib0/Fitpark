Frequently Asked Questions

FIT PARK FAQ

How can I register at Fit Park? +
You can register directly at the gym with a valid ID and a photo, or contact us by phone at +216 93 000 303 to schedule an appointment. A free trial is offered to all new members.
What are your opening hours? +
The gym is open Monday to Saturday from 7:00 AM to 10:00 PM, and Sunday from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM. Special hours may apply during holidays. Contact us to confirm.
What is the HYROX certification? +
HYROX is a worldwide functional fitness competition combining running and strength exercises. Our Coach Saif is a certified HYROX Performance Coach, making Fit Park the only certified club in the region for HYROX competition preparation.
Do you offer classes for beginners? +
Absolutely! We welcome all levels, from complete beginners to seasoned athletes. Our coaches tailor programs to your level and goals. A free fitness assessment is offered to every new member.
Is there a minimum membership commitment? +
We offer monthly memberships with no mandatory long-term commitment, as well as quarterly and annual packages at preferential rates. Contact us to learn about current offers and available promotions.
Do you offer nutritional guidance? +
Yes, nutritional guidance is included in our Champion plan. It is also available as an add-on for other plans. Our coaches will guide you on the nutritional basics tailored to your fitness goals.
